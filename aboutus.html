About

Find Out More About Us

We have been one of the eminent logistics based service providers in the country for a long time.

Pioneers in delivering the finest quality products, Logistics league (Pvt) Ltd is currently in the forefront in terms of leading the Sri Lankan export industry forward. As the main exporters of commodities such as locally produced spices, fresh and organic fruits and vegetables, we ensure that our clients reap the best benefit in terms of quality as well as quantity. Our locally grown spices are accompanied with  naturally infused strong and aromatic flavors, which is the delicious secret behind the Sri Lankan culinary art expertise. We also take pride in exporting fresh fruits and vegetables of the finest quality void of chemicals, artificial fertilizers and genetic modification. As a tropical country gifted with a rich vegetation all year round, we find pride in being able to cater to our huge clientele with the healthiest supply of organic fruits and vegetables at reasonable rates throughout the year. As the main exporters of commodities such as spices, fruits and vegetables, especially to the Middle Eastern market, the Company has made its mark in the export industry within a very short span since its inception. Beyond the scope of food commodities, the Company is also engaged in the business of importing and exporting a wider range of equipments that include power tools and machineries, hardware items, furniture, cosmetics and etc. With hands on experience in imports and exports clearances, we provide an excellent service to our clients based on their diversified requirements. A registered Company with the Sri Lanka Export Development Board, Logistics league (Pvt) Ltd has always been committed to delivering an outstanding and diligent service since its establishment as a business in 2019.

Our Vision.

We as a service oriented Company are always dedicated to cater to our clients’ needs with a friendly and hassle free approach. While we wish to deliver an optimum service in terms of importing and exporting our products to our valued customers at reasonable rates, our primary goal is to ensure customer satisfaction at all times.
